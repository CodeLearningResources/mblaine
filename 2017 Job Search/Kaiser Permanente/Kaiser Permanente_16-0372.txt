Title	Financial Analyst
City	Rockville
State	MD
Description	BASIC FUNCTIONS
Extracts, manipulates, and distributes information from data systems to service area leaders. Supports and prepares comprehensive analyses, operational and financial reports with written summaries to organizational leadership.

RESPONSIBILITIES
• Extracts data from multiple systems using tools such as SQL, SAS, MS Access and Crystal Reports.
• Compiles and generates reports from KPHC, Clarity, and workforce management data systems.
• Performs data analysis and provides consultative and advisory recommendations to service area leaders to support data requirements for strategic operational and financial decisions.
• Compiles and distributes financial data provided by regional operations and finance staff. 
• Maintains the budget system and manages department submissions for service area overall budget. 
• Validates budget information against HR systems and KPHC. 
• Participates in the planning and development of service area, departmental and/or functional budgets. 
• Participates in and/or leads projects of moderate to complex scope as assigned.
• Conducts ongoing user education and provides management consultation on the use and interpretation of analyses, reports, methodologies and source data and systems. 
• May develop spreadsheet and database analyses for presentation and analysis of financial reports. 
• Monitors compliance with policies and procedures related to financial management and budget systems and related automated systems. Travel within the service area and region as required.
• Supports compliance and the Principles of Responsibility (Kaiser Permanente’s code of conduct) by maintaining confidentiality, protecting the assets of the organization, acting with ethics and integrity, reporting non-compliance, and adhering to applicable Federal and State laws and regulations, accreditation and licensure requirements, and Kaiser Permanente policies and procedures.
Requirements	QUALIFICATIONS
• Bachelor's degree in a finance, accounting, or related field, CPA or Master’s Degree preferred. 
• Four plus years related experience in financial analysis and budgeting. 
• Proficient in data retrieval and manipulation from various applications including MS Excel, Access, VBA, SQL, SAS and KP Health Connect. 
• Able to formulate operational study designs and prepare and conduct professional presentations. 
• Advanced proficiency in PC based spreadsheet applications, including advanced functions such as graphics, pivot tables, macros and database management. 
• Demonstrated experience in translating business user needs into accurate management reports.
• Highly developed knowledge of finance theories such as presents values theory/cash flow analysis. 
• Experience working with departments to develop resource needs within the operational plan and budgeting framework.
• Strong verbal, written and presentation communication skills. Strong facilitation skills.
• Works under limited guidance; accountable to complete work assignments.
• Travel to the various medical office building may be required.
